Mozilla Firefox is a free and open-source web browser developed by the Mozilla Foundation. It is a popular browser that is available on a variety of platforms, including Windows, Mac, Linux, and Android. Firefox is known for its speed, security, and flexibility, as well as its support for a wide range of web standards.

One of the key features of Firefox is its support for extensions, which are small pieces of software that can be installed to add new functionality to the browser. There are thousands of extensions available for Firefox, ranging from ad blockers and privacy tools to games and language translation tools. This allows users to customize Firefox to suit their needs and preferences.
